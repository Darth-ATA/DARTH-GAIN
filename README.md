# DARTH-GAIN

_The dark side of progressive overload._

Automate your Hevy workout progression for hypertrophy gains. DARTH-GAIN ingests your Hevy workout data daily, applies principled hypertrophy progression rules (double progression, deload detection, volume management), and updates your routines — no manual spreadsheet work, no ChatGPT copy-paste.

## The Philosophy

DARTH-GAIN is deterministic and transparent. Every weight suggestion is the result of explicit rules you can inspect, tune, and trust. No black boxes, no LLM flakiness, no subscription costs — just principled progressive overload running on your homelab.
